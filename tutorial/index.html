SalmoNet workflows and tutorials

SalmoNet is a molecular interaction database focussing on mapping the intracellular interactions of Salmonella strains. It hosts the integrated networks of 20 Salmonella strains, containing protein-protein interaction, transcriptional regulatory, and enzyme-enzyme interactions. More information is available in our recent manuscript.

The interactions can be queried through the NDEx platform or can be downloaded through the website.

SalmoNet tutorials and workflows

This is a collection of tutorials, guides and workflows, which aim to help the users carry out their own analyses using Salmonella interaction information.
